ABOUT SALISBURY FIRE AND RESCUE The Salisbury Fire Department is a fully volunteer department consisting of 20+ Fire Fighters. The mission of our Fire Department is to provide fire protection and rescue services for the citizens and property located in the village of Salisbury as well as surrounding mutual aid areas.
ABOUT SALISBURY The Town of Salisbury (population approx. 2000) is located in Southeast NB. We are western neighbors to Moncton and Riverview. With the Trans Canada Highway and CN railway passing through our community, the Salisbury Fire Dept has to be ready for any type of emergency.

Our coverage area also covers a number of outlying communities such as Riverglade, Colpitts Settlement, Parkindale, Upper Coverdale and Boundary Creek. We are also part of a mutual aid agreement with Petitcodiac, Havelock, Elgin, Penobsquis, Riverview and Moncton. These neighboring departments assist with larger scale emergencies and water shuttle. Salisbury returns the favour by responting to neighboring departments emergencies.
